Interview

participation of: Danica Ongcamua - 12 shs

What is Fashion?

-Fashion, for me, is our own kind of style. It is not about just the beautiful dresses we wear. But instead, it is something that we are comfortable to wear.

What kind of style you really love street wear or the chick style?

- I always prefer chick style. My choices in fashion match this style because I am more confident when I'm wearing dress, skirt or shorts.

Do you prefer branded clothes or not?

- For me, I choose the things I wear depending on its style and durability. So even if it is branded or not, I will wear it if it has a good quality.

What color you must prefer the lighter one or a little bit darker? 

- Both, it depends on my mood and occasions I will go to. And it is easier to pick a color because I have a fair skin tone.

What advice can you give to other people who's still finding their style? 

- Find the clothes you are most comfortable to wear. Never choose a style that is awkward for you to wear. Best styles come from comfortability
